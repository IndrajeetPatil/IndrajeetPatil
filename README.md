My career spans an uncommon arc – from **physics** to **neuroscience** to **software engineering** – and the thread is not any single discipline but **learning how to learn**. Each transition required building fluency from scratch in an unfamiliar field, which has sharpened my ability to **identify deep structural patterns across domains** and bring outside perspectives to technical problems. In an era where code itself is increasingly commoditized, I believe the differentiating capability is not writing software but **thinking clearly about what to build and why** – translating ambiguous business and scientific problems into well-reasoned architectures and solutions. With 10+ years of engineering experience across research, pharma, and energy, I bring both the **execution depth** to deliver production systems and the **cross-domain breadth** to ask the questions that programmers working within a single domain often miss.
