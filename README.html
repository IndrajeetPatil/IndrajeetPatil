About Me

I am passionate about software design and development, especially about minimizing software complexity and writing readable, maintainable, and performant code. Formerly, I carried out research in social psychology and neuroscience for 10 years, and have extensive experience in data analysis, visualization, reporting, and communication. My research focused on the neural and psychological basis of moral decision-making (see publications). 🥼🔬👨‍💻
